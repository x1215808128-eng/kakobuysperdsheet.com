Most beginners do not struggle with Kakobuy itself — they struggle with **finding the right product link** before they ever reach the agent checkout. That is exactly what the **Kakobuy Spreadsheet** solves. Instead of scrolling Taobao in Chinese or guessing which Weidian seller is reliable, you start from a curated product library, copy a verified link, and run the standard Kakobuy purchase flow from there.

This guide is based on how the Kakobuy Spreadsheet works on [KakobuyPlus](https://www.kakobuyplus.com/en/products) in 2026, combined with the actual ordering steps documented in Kakobuy's buying workflow.

## What the Kakobuy Spreadsheet Actually Is

A **Kakobuy Spreadsheet** is not a single Google Sheet anymore — at least not for most buyers in 2026. The better version is a structured product site that organizes Taobao, Weidian, and 1688 finds into searchable categories with USD pricing and QC references.

On KakobuyPlus, the spreadsheet currently lists **110,000+ products** across categories like Clothing, Shoes, Accessories, Bags, and Electronics. Each listing is meant to help you:

- Skip random seller hunting
- Compare price before opening Kakobuy
- Shortlist items with known batches or popular finds
- Move faster from browsing to cart

Important: the spreadsheet is a **discovery layer**, not the store. KakobuyPlus does not process payment. You still complete the purchase inside **Kakobuy**, which acts as your buying agent, warehouse, QC checkpoint, and international shipper.

## Before You Start: Set Up Kakobuy First

Do this before browsing the spreadsheet, otherwise you will collect links you cannot order immediately.

1. **Create a Kakobuy account**
2. **Top up your balance** using a supported method such as PayPal, card, or other available payment options
3. **Open two tabs** — KakobuyPlus in one tab, Kakobuy in the other

This sounds basic, but it prevents the most common beginner mistake: saving 15 product links, then realizing you still need to register, recharge, and learn the cart flow under pressure.

## Step 2: Shortlist Only 3–5 Items for Your First Haul

This is one of the most useful real-world rules from experienced buyers: **your first haul should teach you the process, not stress your budget**.

A good first haul usually looks like:

- 1 pair of shoes **or** 1 jacket
- 2 tees or hoodies
- 1 small accessory

That keeps weight lower, shipping cheaper, and QC review manageable. Many guides recommend keeping a first package around **under 4 kg** so you learn freight costs without committing too much inventory at once.

Also: leave items in your cart for 24 hours. A lot of spreadsheet finds look amazing at night and much less necessary the next morning.

## Step 3: Verify the Listing Before Copying the Link

The spreadsheet helps you narrow choices, but it does not replace your own check.

Before copying any link, verify:

| Check | What to look for |
| --- | --- |
| Link status | Product page still opens correctly |
| Price | Spreadsheet price roughly matches listing price |
| Size info | Size chart, "size up once," or measurement notes |
| QC reference | Existing buyer QC is better than seller photos only |
| Platform | Taobao, 1688, Weidian, or Tmall — this changes how Kakobuy accepts the link |
| Seller activity | Recent sales or comments are better than dead listings |

A row labeled "best batch" does not mean perfect for **your** size, country, or budget. Treat spreadsheet tags as starting points, not guarantees.

## Step 4: Copy the Link and Submit It to Kakobuy

This is where many beginners get stuck, because **Kakobuy does not handle every platform the same way**.

### If the product is from Taobao, 1688, Tmall, or JD

These platforms are directly supported in Kakobuy's search bar.

1. Copy the product URL from the spreadsheet listing
2. Paste it into the **Kakobuy search box**
3. Let Kakobuy pull the item data automatically
4. Select color, size, and quantity
5. Add to cart or buy now

If Kakobuy loads the item correctly, this is the fastest path.

### If the product is from Weidian, Yupoo, Xianyu, or WeChat sellers

This is critical: many spreadsheet finds come from **Weidian**, and Kakobuy often cannot auto-parse those links the same way.

For these products, use **Looking for Goods** / manual order entry and fill in:

- Product URL
- Product name
- Price
- Color
- Size
- Quantity
- Notes if needed

According to Kakobuy buying guides, Weidian and similar platforms usually require this manual route rather than a simple paste-and-search workflow. If you skip this step, you will think a link is "broken" when it actually just needs manual submission.

### If Kakobuy shows "Fill in Purchase Order Page"

Sometimes even supported links open a manual purchase form. That usually means Kakobuy could not fully crawl the listing. Fill in the product details carefully and double-check the price before paying.

## Step 5: Add Agent Notes Before You Pay

Before checkout, add notes when the item matters on details:

- `Please check logo placement`
- `Confirm EU 43 size tag`
- `Measure chest width before shipping`
- `Check print alignment on back`

This is especially important for clothing and printed items. A 10-second note can save a bad purchase.

Also watch for these platform-specific issues:

- **1688** may have MOQ / minimum order quantity
- **Weidian** often needs manual order entry
- Some items have **shipping restrictions** depending on country and line

## Step 6: Pay Product Cost + Domestic Shipping

When you submit the order, you are paying for:

- Item price
- Domestic shipping inside China
- Kakobuy service fees according to the current platform rules

After payment, Kakobuy typically reviews the order within about **3–5 working hours**, then purchases from the seller. Domestic delivery to the Kakobuy warehouse usually takes around **3–5 days**, though it can vary by seller.

You can track this under **My Orders** in Kakobuy.

## Step 7: Review Warehouse QC Photos Before You Ship

Once the item arrives at the warehouse, Kakobuy uploads **QC photos** of the actual product you bought.

This is the most important step in the entire process.

Compare the warehouse photos against:

- The spreadsheet image
- The original seller listing
- Any QC references you used when selecting the item

Zoom in on:

- Logo placement
- Stitching and seams
- Sole shape and color
- Print quality
- Zipper, tags, and hardware
- Size label accuracy

If the item looks wrong, resolve it **before** international shipping. After you approve shipment, fixes become much harder.

## Step 8: Rehearse Packaging and Estimate Freight

Before paying international shipping, experienced buyers often use rehearsal / pre-pack options when available so they know the real weight and box size.

Why this matters:

- Shipping is often charged by **actual weight** after packing
- Removing shoe boxes can save a lot of volume
- A haul that looked cheap on paper can become expensive at freight step

Kakobuy also offers around **100 days of free warehouse storage**, which helps if you want to order a few items first and combine them into one shipment later.

## Step 9: Choose Shipping Line and Pay International Freight

Go to **My Warehouse**, select the items you want to ship, and choose a logistics line.

Consider:

| Factor | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Speed | Faster lines cost more |
| Weight | Shoes and jackets increase cost quickly |
| Insurance | Worth it for expensive items |
| Customs | Depends on your country |
| Box removal | Can reduce volumetric weight |

If the final weight is lower than estimated, the shipping difference is usually refunded after dispatch.

## Step 10: Track, Receive, and Confirm

After dispatch:

1. Track the parcel in **My Account → Package**
2. Inspect the box on arrival
3. Compare received items with the QC photos you approved
4. Confirm receipt in Kakobuy after delivery

If something is wrong, contact support quickly with photos.

## Real Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Treating the spreadsheet like a guarantee

A find can be popular and still wrong for your size or expectations.

### 2. Using Weidian links like Taobao links

If a Weidian item fails in search, switch to **Looking for Goods** instead of assuming the link is dead.

### 3. Shipping before QC review

Never approve international shipping without checking warehouse photos.

### 4. Building too big a first haul

Start small. Learn cart → QC → freight once before scaling up.

### 5. Ignoring total cost

A low CNY price does not mean a low final delivered cost. Include domestic shipping, agent fees, and international freight in your budget.

## FAQ

### Is the Kakobuy Spreadsheet beginner-friendly?

Yes — if you use it as a **shortlist tool**, not a guarantee. The easiest first workflow is: browse on KakobuyPlus, order 3–5 items, learn QC, then scale.

### Do spreadsheet photos always match the final item?

No. Sellers change batches, listings update, and spreadsheet thumbnails can be older than the current stock. Warehouse QC matters more than the preview image.

### Can I use spreadsheet links with other agents?

Usually yes for Taobao, Weidian, and 1688 links. But this guide is optimized for the Kakobuy workflow, especially if you are browsing the Kakobuy Spreadsheet and ordering through Kakobuy.

### What is the safest first haul size?

Many buyers recommend staying near **3–5 items** and around **under 4 kg** for the first package. That is enough to learn the process without too much risk.
